ABOUT

Scott Anderson spent 15 years in AAA game development as an Art Director and Lighting Designer at Crystal Dynamics, shaping cinematic visuals for titles including Tomb Raider: Underworld, Whiplash, and the Gex series. His work focused on lighting, materials, concept art, and 3D environments—building immersive worlds frame by frame.

After leaving games, Scott spent another 15 years as a fine artist, showing textural oil paintings in galleries across California, Arizona, and Nevada. That chapter sharpened his eye for color, composition, and the emotional weight of visual storytelling.

Today, those two careers converge at SRA Motion Studio. Scott creates cinematic AI videography for brands that need high-impact content at social media speed—combining the technical precision of game development with the artistic sensibility of a painter's eye.
