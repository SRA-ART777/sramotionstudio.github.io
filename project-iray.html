Nightfall iRay USA

Product • Narrative Concept

We all start somewhere. This was my first AI videography reel—the concept was ambitious, the original cut too long. This trimmed version captures the key moments: the iRay logo morphs from a sketchbook drawing as the sea transforms into a stormy ocean, then we follow a hog through a dark forest before ending in a Mordor-inspired landscape where the all-seeing eye reveals itself as the Eye of iRay. Yes, out there. Built in Nano Banana and Kling.
